Athletic Requirements

Parents, To participate in Waipahu High School's Athletic Program, ALL of the following information listed below must be completed. The forms and videos are provided for your convenience. If you need hard copies, please stop by our front office.

Forms 1-3 need to be turned in:

  1. Signed Athletic Physical Form by Doctor and Parent
  2. Signed Assumption of Risk Form
  3. AAF (Athletic Acknowledgement Form)

Parents will need to watch/read:

  1. Risk Management Video
  2. Parent/Student - DOE Parent Handbook
  3. Concussion Education

Student requirements with school needed:

  1. Completed PTP (ALL 12th Graders)
  2. ALL Dues Paid
  3. NO outstanding Obligations
