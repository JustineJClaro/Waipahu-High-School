Need a Sport Physical?

Here's where you can go

  1. Call Waipahu Health Room (Cheryl Ripp) to book an appointment at their earliest opening. 808-307-9541
  2. Your primary care doctor (should be free or very inexpensive with insurance)
  3. CVS minute clinic (costs $70)
  4. Urgent Care (call to ask if they accept insurance for these)
  5. No doctor? Check out Waipahu Family Health Center Monday-Friday, 7:15am-4:30pm, Ph: 8086973888 94-428 Mokuola Street Suite 108B & 213 Waipahu, Hawaii 96797
Tickets for Games If you need tickets for an away game, you must look at the school we are challenging for the tickets. It will not be listed on the waipahu site. If you need tickets for games at Waipahu, go to www.gofan.com and look for Waipahu events.
All Sports Turn in physicals and paperwork to the training room or please drop off in the front office in the trainers box. Coaches will be notified if you are cleared to practice Workouts are for those who have submitted physicals and paperwork to the front office and ALL sections are filled out, signed and dated or it will be returned (note Voluntary Concussion Management Study needs to be completed), it is voluntary to participate in the study, but mandatory to fill out the section. Please make sure your dues is paid for in the front office. Also if you haven't contacted your sport specific coach, coaches emails are listed under coaching staff. Please email them ot let them know you want to sign up and if you have any questions.
